Chest + Upper Body Power

Finish the week with heavier pressing, shoulders, triceps, dips, and a rowing-machine finisher.

Back to FitnessRestart Monday
6Training blocks
PowerUpper body focus
Row15 min finisher

Workout

Use the 5 x 5 bench press as the main strength anchor, then build upper-body volume around it.

  • Bench Press5 x 5
  • Incline Dumbbell Press4 x 8
  • Shoulder Press4 x 10
  • Tricep Pushdown3 x 12
  • Dips3 x max reps
  • Rowing machine15 min

Focus Notes

The aim is controlled power: strong sets, clean technique, and a conditioning finish.

Bench

Rest enough between heavy sets so the 5 x 5 stays strong.

Shoulders

Press smoothly and keep the ribs down to avoid over-arching.

Row

Finish with steady effort on the rower rather than sprinting too early.
