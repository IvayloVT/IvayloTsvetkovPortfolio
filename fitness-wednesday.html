A second chest day with more volume and core work to build control, endurance, and better definition.

Workout

Use smooth reps and keep the core circuit controlled rather than rushed.

  • Incline Bench Press4 x 8
  • Dumbbell Bench Press4 x 10
  • Pec Deck3 x 15
  • Decline Push-ups3 x max reps
  • Core CircuitPlank, leg raises, Russian twists, crunches

Focus Notes

This day is about quality volume and keeping the body tight through each movement.

Volume

Do not chase maximum weight. Let the reps build the training effect.

Core

Brace through each core movement and keep the lower back controlled.

Recovery

Eat well after this session so Thursday legs are not compromised.
