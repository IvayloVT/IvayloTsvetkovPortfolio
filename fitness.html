5-Day Training Program

A PureGym-style weekly split focused on fat loss, chest development, stamina, and stronger overall conditioning.

Monday

Heavy Chest + Cardio

  • 10 min treadmill incline walk
  • Barbell Bench Press - 4 x 8
  • Incline Dumbbell Press - 4 x 10
  • Chest Press Machine - 3 x 12
  • Cable Fly - 3 x 15
  • Push-ups - 3 x max reps
  • 15 min incline walk or Stairmaster
 Tuesday

Pull + Fat Burn

  • Lat Pulldown - 4 x 10
  • Seated Row - 4 x 12
  • Dumbbell Curl - 3 x 12
  • Hammer Curl - 3 x 12
  • Face Pull - 3 x 15
  • HIIT: 30 sec sprint, 90 sec slow pace x 10 rounds
 Wednesday

Chest Volume + Core

  • Incline Bench Press - 4 x 8
  • Dumbbell Bench Press - 4 x 10
  • Pec Deck - 3 x 15
  • Decline Push-ups - 3 x max reps
  • Core circuit: plank, leg raises, Russian twists, crunches
 Thursday

Legs + Cardio

  • Leg Press - 4 x 12
  • Squats - 4 x 10
  • Romanian Deadlift - 3 x 10
  • Leg Curl - 3 x 12
  • Calf Raises - 4 x 15
  • 20-25 min incline walk
 Friday

Chest + Upper Power

  • Bench Press - 5 x 5
  • Incline Dumbbell Press - 4 x 8
  • Shoulder Press - 4 x 10
  • Tricep Pushdown - 3 x 12
  • Dips - 3 x max reps
  • 15 min rowing machine

Nutrition Guide

Simple food choices and habits to support weight loss while building muscle.

  • Protein target: 1.6-2.2g per kg of bodyweight
  • Drink 2-3L water daily
  • Stay in a calorie deficit for fat loss
  • Aim for 7-8 hours of sleep
  • Optional: creatine, whey protein, black coffee before training
Breakfast: Oats, banana, Greek yogurt
Lunch: Chicken, rice, vegetables
Snack: Protein shake and fruit
Dinner: Salmon or chicken with potatoes and salad
Late snack: Greek yogurt or boiled eggs
